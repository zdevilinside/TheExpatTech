## The $2 Miracle

The mechanic aired up the tire, but a loud hiss revealed a dented rim. Smiling, he jacked up the van, removed the tire, hammered the rim flat with a block of wood, and reinflated it. It held at about 80%. Good enough.  

When I asked what I owed, he waved me off. Insisting, I followed him inside. He grabbed a Coke, gestured for me to take a water, and charged me only for the drinks. **Total cost: about $2.**  

Angel was stunned when I told her. We were back on the road, grateful for Mexican hospitality.

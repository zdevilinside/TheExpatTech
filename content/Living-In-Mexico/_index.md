## Living in Mexico

Since August 2015, I’ve been navigating life here—learning the culture, handling the paperwork, and adapting to everyday realities. This section shares those experiences, from immigration and residency to daily life quirks and lessons learned along the way.  

You’ll find practical tips, personal stories, and reflections on what it really means to settle into Mexico.

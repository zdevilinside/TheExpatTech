14. **Separate browsing contexts.**  
   Use different browsers or profiles for work and personal use. Prevent cookie and session cross-contamination.

   - **Multi-hop connections**: Add layers of protection when handling highly sensitive data.

20. **Monitor performance and adapt.**  
   VPNs can slow connections. Test speeds before client calls, switch servers strategically, and know when to use split tunneling for efficiency.

21. **Understand the limits of commercial VPN servers.**  
   Commercial VPN providers often use shared IP addresses. These IPs quickly become known to streaming services, financial institutions, and even government firewalls. As a result, you may find yourself blocked or flagged simply because too many users are funneling through the same server.

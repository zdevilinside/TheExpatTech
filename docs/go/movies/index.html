Getting started with MegaOTT is simple. Visit our website, choose a subscription plan that suits your needs, and follow the on-screen instructions to set up MegaOTT on your preferred device.

MegaOTT is compatible with a variety of devices, including smart TVs, streaming devices, and mobile devices through our free app. For a detailed list of supported devices, please visit our Device Compatibility section on the website.

To become a reseller, sign up for our reseller program through our website. Once registered, you can access the reseller dashboard to manage subscriptions, credits, and interact with your customers.

If you encounter any streaming issues, first ensure your internet connection is stable. If the problem persists, contact our support team through the website for further assistance.

ZDevilInside published on 2015-08-01 included in Living in Mexico

Moving to Mexico was a rushed endeavor for us. We were moving down to Mexico in a used minivan that we bought just for the move. You cannot rent a U-Haul to move across international borders. The van we bought has cheap and the plan was to get to Mexico and then just get rid of it. That turns out not to be something you can do.

To say we were not properly prepared for this move is an understatement of the century. We were basing things on what we knew about living in the United States, and from our couple of trips to Puerto Penasco, Mexico AKA Rocky Point, or, as my girlfriend at the time kept calling Pina Colada because she was drunk. Needless to say, things changed!

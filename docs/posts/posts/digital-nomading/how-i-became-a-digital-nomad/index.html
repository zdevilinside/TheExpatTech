How I became a digital nomad?

ZDevilInside included in Digital Nomading
 2015-07-15  103 words   One minute 
Contents

I was on my way to graduate school in Germany back in 2015. I got as far as Amsterdam when I ended up being hospitalized for 15 days. 7 days in was when graduate school started. The Germans wouldn’t let me have a late start so I had to return to the US. We had already sold our house by then so I needed to move somewhere fast. My requirements were that the place was inexpensive, had great internet service, was near a major international airport, and that “snow” was a foreign language and the only ice you got was in your drink.
